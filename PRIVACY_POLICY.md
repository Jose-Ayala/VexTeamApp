# Privacy Policy for VexTeamApp

**Last Updated: January 2026**

This application is designed to help VEX Robotics teams track their progress. Your privacy is important to us.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). The app does not require a user account, email address, or access to your device's contacts or location.

## 2. External API Usage
The app communicates with the RobotEvents API to display public competition data. This includes team names, event results, awards, and skills rankings. This data is publicly available and is not modified by this application.

## 3. Local Storage
The app uses local storage (SharedPreferences) only to remember the last Team ID you searched for. This information remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## 4. Internet Permissions
The app requires internet access solely to communicate with the RobotEvents API to fetch the latest team statistics.
